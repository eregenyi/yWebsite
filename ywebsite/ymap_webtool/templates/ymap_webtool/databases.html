The yMap website hosts data about full yeast genome which is use as reference to map the mutations.

The yeast post translational modifications (PTMs) are obtained from PTMcode 2.0 and PTMfunc.

The protein features (domains, nucleotide binding domains, structural regions, active and binding sites) are obtained from UniProt.

Ymap uses data from the Saccharomyces Genome Database (SGD) to translate mutations at DNA level to protein level.

These data are updated on a regular basis.
