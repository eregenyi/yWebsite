The yMOOSE server hosts several datasets downloaded from several reference databases.

The translation of the mutation from DNA level to protein level and the identification of non-synonymous SNPs is performed using the complete Saccharomyces S. cerevisiae's annotated sequence from the Saccharomyces Genome Database (SGD)

Functional and structural annotations of the complete Saccharomyces S. cerevisiae proteome along with the position of modified post-translational modified residues are obtained from the UniProt KB database.

The list of BioGRID IDs for the complete Saccharomyces S. cerevisiae yeast proteome is is also obtained from the UniProt KB database.

More detailed PTM annotations are obtained from PTMcode 2.0 and PTMfunc.
