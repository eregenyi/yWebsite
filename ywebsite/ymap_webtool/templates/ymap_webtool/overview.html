yMap is a python based fast and robust automated method to map large yeast variants data to proteins post-translational modifications, proteins domains, proteins-nucleotide binding domains, proteins structural regions, proteins active and binding sites proteins networks visualisation.

The protein features are collected from Uniprot.

The post-translational modifications are collected from PTMcode 2.0 and PTMfunc.

yMap generates a "final-report" which reports all the non-synonymous mutations that overlaps or falls inside the above mentioned proteins functional regions. The final-report is complemented GO enrichment analysis and biogrid visualisation id.
