YMap facilitates the interpretation of large scale variant data and the correlation of genotype to phenotype.
It enables the mapping of large sets of mutations to the structural/functional protein features:

    Protein features from UniProt KB database:

    • Protein active and binding sites
    • Protein domains
    • Protein-nucleotide binding domains
    • Protein structural regions
    • Protein post-translational modifications

    Post translational modifications from PTMfunc:

    • Acetylation present on a residue present at the protein interface
    • Acetylation present on a residue important for protein-protein interaction
    • Ubiquitination present on a residue present at the protein interface
    • Ubiquitination present on a residue important for protein-protein interaction
    • Phosphorylation present on a residue present at the protein interface
    • Phosphorylation present on a residue important for protein-protein interaction

    Post translational modifications from PTMcode 2.0:

    • PTM located in a regulatory hotspot (as defined by Beltrao et al. 2012)
    • PTM involved in interaction with an other PTM within the same protein
    • PTM involved in nteraction with an other PTM located on an other protein
It also performs a GO enrichment analysis and provides protein's BioGRID ids.
